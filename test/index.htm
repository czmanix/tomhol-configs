Camoodle Instructions click to expand contents

Camoodle displays your images on your smart phone. Flick your wrist to see another image.

To add an image to your smart phone, click the Capture button or enter the URL of an image below. Choose a picture or select one from your photo library, and it will appear on the left.

The colorized or black-and-white image that will appear on your phone is shown on the right. There is a faint overlay where the date and time appear on your Pebble watch; it is smaller if you choose to only display the time and not the date.

There are tools listed to help you create the best-possible image for your Pebble. Drag within the image to align it, and use the slider for zooming. The Reset, Flip, and Rotate buttons are also helpful.

When you are ready, click OK to send the image to your Pebble.

More custom settings are available when you choose the Settings button, and the Clear button will remove all images on your Pebble so you can create a new set.

Unfortunately capturing an image in Camoodle only works on iOS devices.
